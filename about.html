Tang Moon

Real Estate Agent and Financial Coach

Who is Tang?!?11/

Tang is a friendly civet that looks like a Miqo'te. Being a civet, she has a certain musk that can be interpreted as pleasant or repulsive depending on the person. She's very self conscious of this and covers it up with lots of soap, showers, deodorants, and perfumes at the same time.

Her dreams include being on a Homeowner's Association board of directors, or being a clown.

She also owns a Sari-Sari store!

Please give money!!!!

Tang, her sister Sunny, and her friends Tian Peng and Fortunata Peng, come from an unknown island near the Far East.

She's not yet completely familiar with all of these customs of Eorzea, but accepts all forms of currency!

What about Whiskey!11

Whiskey is also from the unknown island. He's not quite a Namazu either! He can turn small amounts of ginger into gold.

He is banned from his local Alcoholics Anonymous group for inciting a rebellion.
